About BYTE&BITE

Byte & Bite is the official online ordering platform for the Tuck Shop at MIT ADT University. We make it easy for students to order, pay, and pick up delicious food—fast, fresh, and hassle-free.

The Tuck Shop at MIT ADT

Located at the heart of MIT ADT University, the Tuck Shop is a vibrant hub for students to grab a quick bite, enjoy a meal with friends, or recharge between classes. Our menu is curated to offer a variety of delicious, affordable, and healthy options for everyone.

  • Freshly prepared meals every day
  • Buffet, pay-and-eat, and canteen options
  • Online ordering and cashless payment
  • Quick pickup and real-time order tracking

Proudly serving the MIT ADT community with love and care!

Our Mission

To empower students with a seamless, digital-first food experience—making campus life tastier, easier, and more connected. We believe in great food, great service, and great community.
